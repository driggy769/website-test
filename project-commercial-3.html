3XS Custom Systems

Project Overview: 3XS Custom Builds

Work on 3XS Custom Builds differed from other projects in that it supported a distinct branch of the business with its own identity and objectives. While operating under the wider SCAN umbrella, 3XS functioned as its own department with separate brand guidelines, offering greater creative freedom and scope to explore new concepts.

This additional flexibility allowed for more experimentation in both visual design and user experience, while still ensuring consistency with the established 3XS brand.

My Role & Responsibilities

I was involved in the full lifecycle of 3XS Custom Build pages, from initial concept and design through to development and deployment. This included interpreting briefs, designing page layouts, building interactive front-end features, and managing the transition from staging to live.

Briefing & Concept Development

As with other projects, each 3XS Custom Build began with a brief outlining the goals of the page, required content, and any constraints or brand considerations. However, compared to other areas of the business, these briefs allowed for greater creative input and encouraged exploration of new ideas.

This made the concept phase particularly important, as it was an opportunity to propose enhancements to layout, functionality, and overall presentation that could improve engagement and conversion.

Design & Creative Exploration

Initial designs were created in Adobe Photoshop, following 3XS brand guidelines while allowing room for creative interpretation. At this stage, I had more freedom to influence not only the visual direction but also the functional elements of the page.

This included exploring features such as call transitions, animations, and interactive elements, helping to create a more dynamic and engaging experience compared to standard brand or landing pages.

Development & Staging

Once the mockups were reviewed and signed off, the designs were translated into fully functional pages and built on the dedicated 3XS staging site. This allowed for thorough testing and refinement before public release.

The staging phase ensured that both design intent and interactive elements were accurately implemented and performed as expected.

Deployment & Outcome

After successful testing on staging, the pages were deployed to the live 3XS website. The result was a set of high-quality, brand-aligned pages that showcased custom build offerings while benefiting from enhanced creativity and interactivity.

This project highlighted my ability to work within distinct brand ecosystems, contribute creatively beyond static design, and deliver engaging digital experiences from concept through to launch.
