About Me

I’m a creator and developer with a passion for building immersive, visually rich experiences on the web. I focus on blending clean, maintainable code with thoughtful design to create interfaces that feel polished, modern, and intentional.

Over the years, I’ve worked across a range of projects — from interactive front-end experiences to content-driven platforms designed to scale. I enjoy solving problems at the intersection of creativity and logic, and I’m always exploring new ways to improve performance, accessibility, and user experience.

When I’m not coding, I spend time creating content, experimenting with design systems, and learning new tools that push my workflow forward. This site is a reflection of that journey — a place to showcase work, ideas, and experiments.
